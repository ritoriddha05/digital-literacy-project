# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

- Use strong and unique passwords for all accounts  
- Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)  
- Never share OTP or banking details with anyone  
- Always verify UPI payment requests before approving  
- Do not click on suspicious links or unknown emails  
- Check website URLs carefully before entering details  
- Keep your devices and apps updated regularly  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for financial transactions  
- Log out from shared devices after use  

## 🚨 Report Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in
