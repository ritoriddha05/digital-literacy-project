# Social Media Do's and Don'ts

## ✅ Do's
- Use professional language in posts
- Keep your profile clean and respectful
- Think before sharing content
- Use strong privacy settings
- Share useful and informative content

## ❌ Don'ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content
- Do not spread fake news or misinformation
- Avoid cyberbullying or negative comments
- Do not use unprofessional language
